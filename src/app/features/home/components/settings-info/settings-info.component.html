Aim for approximately 8 glasses of water per day (8 ounces each, totaling about 2 liters or half a gallon).

Most adults require 7-9 hours of sleep per night. Consistency is key, so try to go to bed and wake up at the same times each day, even on weekends.

Adults should aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity most days.

Aiming for at least 30 minutes of outdoor activity each day can be beneficial for vitamin D exposure (weather permitting), mental health, and overall well-being. Outdoor activity can be combined with exercise, such as walking, cycling, or playing sports.
